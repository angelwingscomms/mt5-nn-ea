New features added for the new main feature set

Note:
- These are the features that were newly added to the repo.
- The main set also reuses some older features that already existed before, like `ret1`, `upper_wick_rel`, `lower_wick_rel`, `range_rel`, and `close_in_range`.

1. `spread_abs`
Plain meaning: the raw gap between ask and bid.
Why it helps: it shows how expensive trading is right now.

2. `bar_duration_ms`
Plain meaning: how long the bar took to form, in milliseconds.
Why it helps: fast bars and slow bars often mean very different market behavior.

3. `rsi_9`
Plain meaning: short-speed RSI using 9 bars.
Why it helps: shows fast momentum.

4. `rsi_18`
Plain meaning: medium-speed RSI using 18 bars.
Why it helps: shows momentum with a little more smoothing.

5. `rsi_27`
Plain meaning: slower RSI using 27 bars.
Why it helps: shows broader momentum.

6. `atr_9`
Plain meaning: short ATR using 9 bars.
Why it helps: shows short-term volatility.

7. `atr_18`
Plain meaning: medium ATR using 18 bars.
Why it helps: shows medium volatility.

8. `atr_27`
Plain meaning: slower ATR using 27 bars.
Why it helps: shows broader volatility.

9. `macd_line`
Plain meaning: the main MACD value.
Why it helps: compares fast trend versus slow trend.

10. `macd_signal`
Plain meaning: the smoothed MACD line.
Why it helps: helps show momentum turns more clearly.

11. `macd_hist`
Plain meaning: the gap between MACD and the MACD signal line.
Why it helps: shows whether momentum is getting stronger or weaker.

12. `ema_gap_9`
Plain meaning: 9-bar EMA minus current close.
Why it helps: shows how far price is from a short trend line.

13. `ema_gap_18`
Plain meaning: 18-bar EMA minus current close.
Why it helps: shows medium trend distance.

14. `ema_gap_27`
Plain meaning: 27-bar EMA minus current close.
Why it helps: shows a slower trend distance.

15. `ema_gap_54`
Plain meaning: 54-bar EMA minus current close.
Why it helps: shows a much slower trend distance.

16. `ema_gap_144`
Plain meaning: 144-bar EMA minus current close.
Why it helps: shows very long trend distance.

17. `cci_9`
Plain meaning: 9-bar Commodity Channel Index.
Why it helps: shows whether price is stretched away from normal.

18. `cci_18`
Plain meaning: 18-bar CCI.
Why it helps: gives a smoother stretch signal.

19. `cci_27`
Plain meaning: 27-bar CCI.
Why it helps: shows broader overbought/oversold pressure.

20. `willr_9`
Plain meaning: 9-bar Williams %R.
Why it helps: shows where close sits inside the recent high-low range.

21. `willr_18`
Plain meaning: 18-bar Williams %R.
Why it helps: gives a smoother range-position signal.

22. `willr_27`
Plain meaning: 27-bar Williams %R.
Why it helps: shows broader range position.

23. `mom_9`
Plain meaning: current close minus close 9 bars ago.
Why it helps: shows short momentum.

24. `mom_18`
Plain meaning: current close minus close 18 bars ago.
Why it helps: shows medium momentum.

25. `mom_27`
Plain meaning: current close minus close 27 bars ago.
Why it helps: shows slower momentum.

26. `usdx_pct_change`
Plain meaning: percent change of USDX from the previous bar.
Why it helps: gold often reacts to dollar strength changes.

27. `usdjpy_pct_change`
Plain meaning: percent change of USDJPY from the previous bar.
Why it helps: adds another outside-market clue.

28. `bollinger_width_9`
Plain meaning: width of 9-bar Bollinger Bands.
Why it helps: shows short volatility expansion or squeeze.

29. `bollinger_width_18`
Plain meaning: width of 18-bar Bollinger Bands.
Why it helps: shows medium volatility expansion or squeeze.

30. `bollinger_width_27`
Plain meaning: width of 27-bar Bollinger Bands.
Why it helps: shows broader volatility expansion or squeeze.

31. `hour_sin`
Plain meaning: hour of day turned into a smooth cycle number.
Why it helps: lets the model learn time-of-day effects without a hard jump at midnight.

32. `hour_cos`
Plain meaning: the second half of the smooth hour-of-day cycle.
Why it helps: works with `hour_sin` so time wraps around cleanly.

33. `minute_sin`
Plain meaning: minute of hour turned into a smooth cycle number.
Why it helps: lets the model learn minute timing patterns.

34. `minute_cos`
Plain meaning: the second half of the smooth minute cycle.
Why it helps: works with `minute_sin` so the cycle wraps cleanly.

35. `day_of_week_scaled`
Plain meaning: day of week turned into a small number from 0 to 1.
Why it helps: different weekdays often behave differently.
